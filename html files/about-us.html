About Us

The all-in-one platform for growing your farm

At Cultiv8, we aren't just another agricultural company. Founded in 2023 by two young farmers with dirt under their nails and a shared passion for improving the agro industry and helping farmers grow.

We believe every farmer deserves access to high-quality equipment, healthy seedlings and expert guidance. And we're dedicated to providing the resources and support you need to not only survive, but thrive.
